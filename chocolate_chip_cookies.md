% Chocolate Chip Cookies

# Ingredients

### Wet

- 1 cup Butter
- 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
- 3/4 cup White Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 1 tsp Vanilla
- 2 cups Chocolate Chips

### Dry

- 3 cups Flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt

# Method

1. Mix dry ingredients; Mix wet ingredients; combine. Add chocolate chips.

2. Bake for **10 minutes** _(9 minutes and 20 seconds)_ at **375&deg; F**.
