% Blueberry Muffins

# Ingredients

### Wet

- 6 tbsp Butter
- 1 1/4 cups Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 1 tsp Vanilla
- 1 cup Milk
- 2 cups Blueberries

### Dry

- 3 cups & 2 tbsp Flour
- 1 tsp Salt
- 3 tsp Baking Powder

# Method

1. Mix butter, sugar, vanilla, and eggs; mix flour, salt, and baking powder.

2. Add flour mix and milk alternately to wet mix till combined. Fold in blueberries.

3. Grease pans. Bake for **30 mins** at **375&deg; F**.
