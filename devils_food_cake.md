% Devil's Food Cake

# Ingredients

### Wet

- 1/2 cup Butter
- 1 3/4 cup Sugar
- 1 tsp Vanilla
- 3 Eggs
- 1 1/3 cup Cold Water

### Dry

- 2 1/4 cups Flour
- 1/2 cup Cocoa
- 1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
- 1/4 tsp Salt

# Method

1. Mix dry ingredients; Mix wet ingredients (except for water); mix dry and water alternately into wet.

2. Bake for **35-40 minutes** at **350&deg; F**.
